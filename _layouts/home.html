Current Work

I'm currently a product designer at Code for America. Code for America is a national nonprofit that works with municipal governments to improve digital services. I started as a design Fellow in 2016. The fellowship is a selective program that pairs teams experienced technologists with cities on a year long contract. I worked with the Kansas City, MO Health Department, where my team's mission was to improve access to childhood immunization. We were also tasked with introducing city employees to modern, agile software development practices and user-centered design.

I worked alongside a developer and a project manager. As the sole designer on the team, I participated in all stages of the software development cycle. In addition to leading research, I created all design deliverables, including wireframes, user interface mockups, communications, and marketing materials.
